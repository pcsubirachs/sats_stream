About Studio

Visual content with reach

A visual design studio focusing on animation and illustration for all pixel ratios.

Studio spawned out of the digital design studio Px8 as a result of successful creative projects in the field of animation and illustration. We are passionate creators exploring the contemporary visual trends and translating them into thumb-stopper visual content for social media and the broader marketing needs.

Our 3D and 2D motion skills allow us to create compelling visual content that amplifies the client’s message in a world that consumes across all kinds of screens. We believe that through collaboration and exploration we are able to deliver the next level in visual design and storytelling.
