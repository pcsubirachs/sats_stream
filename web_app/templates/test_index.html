About Bolt Boost

Video content with lightning

A video platform with a focus on monetizing videos for creators by allowing users to pay with lightning over the Bitcoin network.

Bolt Boost spawned out of the first BOLT.FUN Hackathon for lightning apps. We were inspired to create a unique and new way for users and followers to directly support their favorite creators. Whether they are podcasters, musicians, or teachers, any creator can now receive 100% of the value they create.

This is just the beginning. The Bitcoin network coupled with lightning is ushering in a new era of self sovereignty. No more middle men. No more rent seeking. Value for value is here and there's no stopping this train. So jump aboard and start boosting those bolts!
